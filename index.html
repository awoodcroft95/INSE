About This Project

This is a Coursework project for my Introduction to Software Engineering unit at University. This unit is looking at creating software in the industry and the way to do things in the industry such as project management and life cycles. For this project we were tasked with creating a piece of free software to manage projects, this piece of software needed to have the functionality to produce Gantt charts, PERT charts and Work Breakdown Trees/Structures.
